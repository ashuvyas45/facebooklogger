Welcome to Blimp flight

ViaBlimp is a productivity enhancer tool which connect your facebook messenger to your application by just messaging our bot.


You get a unique url on messaging the bot and any ping/hit on this url with data will send this data on your facebook account.

Uses

Webhooks

On messaging the bot, it gives you a unique webhook url which you can use
in places to add webhook for spiking.
When this webhook is hit, you get the pretty json response on your messenger account.

Server Logging Spike

Its really difficult for me to see the logs of deployed code to check
the values of objects and variables. Why not use the viablimp logging
modul to log the message along with sending it
to you on your messenger.Viablimp logging module is available
on PIP and NPM. Ruby gem is under construction!

Running time consuming code!

This tool is a blessing for people whose work involve running time consuming programs.
I use it quite a lot in scraping applications.
An error coming 2 hours after running
or when scraping is completed -> you get a straight message on your messenger.

Make your own Pager duty!

Pager Duty on calls is great! But assume pager duty
for a crash coming on the team messenger group!
You can easily make one for your group
with the webhook url provided. Depending on the parameters set.

Using Webhooks

On messaging the bot, You will get a unique url of the form http://viablimp.com/api/HADAGSU32H

If you send a GET/POST request to this url(Try with Restlet), your facebook messenger will send you this data.
